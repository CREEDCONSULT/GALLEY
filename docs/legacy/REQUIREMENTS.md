# Requirements - ContentFlow AI

## Problem Statement
Small business owners struggle to maintain consistent, SEO-optimized content across blogs and social media. ContentFlow AI automates the "Research -> Write -> Optimize -> Publish" lifecycle.

## Core Personas
- **Oliver (Local Business Owner)**: Needs hands-off SEO growth.
- **Greta (SaaS Solo-Founder)**: Needs authority-building content.
- **Andy (Agency Manager)**: Needs to scale content for clients.

## Phase 1: MVP Features (The "Perfect" Foundation)
### 1. Smart Onboarding
- Business URL capture & automatic website crawling.
- Brand Voice extraction: NLP analysis of existing content to mimic tone/style.
- Initial 30-day content calendar generation based on crawl data.

### 2. Command Center (Dashboard)
- Visual calendar showing pending, scheduled, and published content.
- Drag-and-drop rescheduling.
- High-level SEO metrics (Rankings, Traffic, Content Health).

### 3. AI Research Agent
- Daily automated keyword research via DataForSEO/SurferSEO.
- Competitor gap analysis.
- Content "Brief" generation.

### 4. Interactive Editor
- Split-view editor (Markdown/Rich Text).
- Live SEO Scoring: Keyword density, readability, link suggestions.
- Multi-channel preview (WordPress, Buffer/Social).

## Success Metrics
- Average SEO score > 80/100 for generated content.
- < 10 mins user time per approved content asset.
- Positive traffic trend within 60 days of activation.
